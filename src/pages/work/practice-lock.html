iOS App

View on the App Store

One of the biggest fears of students starting middle school is opening their lockers. Practice Lock alleviates this fear by providing a digital combination lock with which they can practice.

Unlike with a real lock, it’s easy to change Practice Lock’s combination. It supports two different dial sizes and five different colors, and even offers hints if the user makes a common mistake trying to unlock it.
