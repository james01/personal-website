{{ title }}

iOS App • Lead Designer & Front-End Developer

The idea for Orbit came from the realization of how easy it is to lose contact with friends you don’t run into in real life. We figured one of the reasons for this is that it can be awkward to call somebody out of the blue.

Orbit tries to fix this by offering a low-friction way to schedule calls with people, and by reminding you so you don’t miss them.
