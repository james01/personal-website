{{ title }}

iOS App • Lead Designer & CTO

Vibe is a social app for discovering music. It is inspired by the classic experience of “sharing the aux” while driving in a car with friends.

This idea, together with the imagery of a lava lamp, informed much of the identity of the app. The interface is bright and colorful, and the app icon is an aux cord formed in the shape of a heart.

The initial version of Vibe had a number of features, from direct messaging songs to posting a Song of the Day.

After beta testing, we were able to simplify the app to its essence—playing music and sharing music. The redesigned version opens to a list of songs shared by a user’s friends. Tapping the orange “+” button allows them to share a song of their own.
